## 1. The strategic read

The site is **deep on one thing and empty on the rest.** Of 53 published articles, **49 are hiking trip-reports.** That is a strong trail library, but it leaves summer's biggest draws uncovered:

| Gap | Persona who owns it | Current articles |
|---|---|---|
| Festivals & events | Hanne Kristoffersen | ~0 |
| Urban summer / city life | Silje Nygård | 0 |
| Coastal & food culture | Lars Erik Nordvik | 0 |
| Midnight-sun-specific content | Bjørn Haugen | 0 |
| Planning guides (ferries, budget, Allemannsretten, DNT cabins) | Ingrid / cross-zone | 1 (+ 7 "coming-soon" stubs) |

**The summer move:** keep the trail library working through re-features, but point most *new* production at festivals, midnight sun, coastal culture, urban summer, and planning guides. That is where summer search demand and affiliate intent live, and it is where we currently rank for nothing.

**The aurora firewall (non-negotiable):** aurora season is **October–March.** Zero aurora marketing in summer content. The Arctic summer angle is the **midnight sun** (Tromsø ~May 18–Jul 26). Bjørn's season-opener article exists partly to stop summer visitors chasing a sky that physically cannot show them the lights.

**Summer "look" status:** largely already in place. The homepage `Hero.tsx` runs a midnight-sun image with a "Midnight Sun Season (Jun–Aug)" badge, and `Services.tsx` seasonal copy already frames fjord season (May–Sept), midnight sun, and "aurora next season Oct 2026–Mar 2027." Recommended light touches in §5 — no visual rebuild needed.

## 7. Voice guardrails (every piece, every desk)

- No banned brochure words (no "vibrant," "charming," "magical," "hidden gem," "breathtaking," "stunning").
- No em dashes. Full stop or comma instead.
- Lead with a number, a named place, or a date. Run the Florida Test.
- Active voice 90%+. Norwegian terms defined on first use.
- Hiking content states the DNT grade and cites Fjellvettreglene by rule number (Blue grade and up).
- Events state real dates, capacity, ticket window. Sami events are Sami-led with the organizer named.
