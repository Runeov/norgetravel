## 2. Prioritized product/experience shortlist (ranked by demand × affiliate fit)

Ranking favors **cookie-based affiliate products** (fit a content site) over B2B agent-commission cruise lines.

### Tier 1 — lead with these

**1. Electric / zero-emission fjord cruises** `summer + year-round`
- *Trend:* mandatory zero-emission UNESCO fjords from Jan 1 2026 (world-first hook); Bergen's stated #1 day trip is the UNESCO Fjord Cruise Flåm & Stegastein (4.7★, ~815 reviews, from $318). "Future of The Fjords" is the world's first all-electric passenger ship (Flåm–Gudvangen).
- *Best affiliate:* **Viator 8–12%** or **GetYourGuide 7%** (both via Awin/in-house; 30-day cookie).
- *Attach to:* existing Nærøyfjord electric ferry + Norway in a Nutshell articles; planned Geiranger-at-peak piece.

**2. Summer fjord day-trips: RIB, kayak, sea-eagle safari** `peak-summer`
- *Trend:* coolcation surge; Lofoten RIB Trollfjord Sea Eagle Safari 4.9★ 800+ reviews from ~$129; midnight-sun kayaking is summer-only inside the Arctic Circle.
- *Best affiliate:* **GetYourGuide 7% / Viator 8–12%**.
- *Attach to:* existing Geiranger RIB + kayak reports, Lofoten trip-reports.

**3. Travel insurance — SafetyWing** `year-round, highest LTV`
- *Trend:* high-intent on every international trip; ~70% sign-ups are subscriptions, so payout compounds.
- *Best affiliate:* **~10% of premium, RECURRING on all renewals, 364-day cookie** (verified-by-corroboration; the manifest figure holds). The single best lifetime-value product found.
- *Attach to:* every planning guide, itinerary, gear/prep article, "what a Norway trip costs."

**4. Northern Lights tours — forward-book now for winter 2026–27** `winter product, summer intent`
- *Trend:* Solar Cycle 25 elevated through 2026–27; industry guidance says book 6–12 months ahead, Tromsø heavily booked.
- *Best affiliate:* **GetYourGuide 7% / Viator 8–12%**.
- *Attach to:* existing "Best time to see the Northern Lights"; autumn aurora-prep content. Capture summer browsers booking ahead.

### Tier 2 — strong secondary

**5. Car rental — Discover Cars** `summer road-trip`
- *Trend:* self-drive fjord/Arctic road trips ride the coolcation wave.
- *Best affiliate:* **Discover Cars — 365-day cookie, ~$20+/booking, re-earns on repeat bookings** (verified, official page). Headline "70%" is 70% of *their* profit, not booking value.
- *Attach to:* the week-1 Ferries & AutoPASS guide, Trollstigen, Rv55 Sognefjellet, budget piece. The 365-day cookie is the standout.

**6. eSIM — Saily or Airalo** `year-round`
- *Trend:* every international visitor needs data.
- *Best affiliate:* **Saily 12% recurring** (reported) beats **Airalo 10%** (verified, non-recurring) if recurring revenue matters.
- *Attach to:* planning guides, budget piece, arrival/logistics content.

**7. Outdoor gear — Norrøna + Helly Hansen** `year-round, high AOV`
- *Trend:* Norway packing demand led by waterproof hardshell (the #1 cited item); 2026 merino/grid-fabric trend.
- *Best affiliate:* **Norrøna 10% via CJ, 30-day cookie** (verified; manifest accurate; ~€300 AOV) and **Helly Hansen up to 20% for content affiliates via Awin** (verified). Both Norwegian-relevant. **Avoid Amazon for shells/boots** (3% outdoors, 24h cookie).
- *Attach to:* existing "What to pack for Arctic Norway, month by month"; trail guides. Norrøna = the manifest's luxury-trekking vertical.

**8. Svalbard summer expeditions & midnight-sun activities** `peak-summer, luxury`
- *Trend:* 85 Svalbard 2026 cruises listed; HX midnight-sun expeditions from June 2026; Longyearbyen RIB day trips.
- *Best affiliate:* **GetYourGuide / Viator** for day trips (cruises are largely B2B). High price point fits the luxury segment.
- *Attach to:* existing Svalbard hikes; planned "Svalbard in summer" article.

### Tier 3 — opportunistic / accessory

**9. Summer whale watching — Andenes/Vesterålen (NOT Tromsø)** `peak-summer`
- *Accuracy win:* Tromsø orca watching is Nov–Jan (winter); the correct summer location is Andenes/Bleik Canyon (sperm whales, peak summer). GetYourGuide/Viator. Attach to coast content + the planned Gjesvær seabird piece.

**10. Rail — Flåm Railway / Norway in a Nutshell** `year-round`
- Best-selling fjord tour, but **Fjord Tours has no verifiable affiliate program** — monetize the same experience via **GetYourGuide/Viator listings** instead. Attach to existing Flåm Railway + Nutshell articles.

**11. Amazon accessory cluster — midnight-sun sleep mask, DEET/midge protection, power bank** `summer, low-cost high-intent`
- No brand program exists for these, so **Amazon Associates (3–4%, 24h cookie)** is the right tool here only. The blackout sleep mask is a perfect micro-product to attach to the week-1 midnight-sun article (accommodations often lack blackout curtains).

**12. VPN — NordVPN** `digital-nomad vertical`
- **40–100% new sales + 30% recurring renewals** (reported). Lower travel-relevance; opportunistic for the digital-nomad angle in the manifest.

## 4. Hard caveats (do not skip before monetizing)

- **All official affiliate pages were 403-blocked.** Every rate above is corroborated across roundups, not read from source. Re-confirm inside each dashboard at signup. Rates drift.
- **Booking.com "25–40%" is share-of-margin, ~4–8% of booking value.** Never publish the headline tier as if it were booking value.
- **Hurtigruten and Havila are B2B travel-agent commission models, not cookie affiliate** (Havila ~10%, HX incentive-based). They need an agency relationship and don't fit standard affiliate-link content.
- **Fjord Tours has no verifiable affiliate program** — do not publish a rate or link until confirmed directly.
- **Every GetYourGuide URL must be HEAD-checked + title-verified** and the `l#####` location code confirmed against a live search per the project URL rules. Never pattern-guess codes.
- **Whale watching seasonality:** Tromsø = winter orcas; Andenes = summer sperm whales. Get this right in content.
- All outbound affiliate links render `rel="noopener noreferrer sponsored" target="_blank"`.
