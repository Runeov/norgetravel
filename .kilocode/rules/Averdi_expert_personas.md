# PERSONA INTERACTION RULES

## When to Use Which Expert

| Content Type | Lead Persona | Support |
|--------------|--------------|---------|
| Sametinget grants | Grant Specialist | Cultural Bridge |
| Tax strategy | Senior Authority | Young Innovator |
| Startup advice | Young Innovator | Senior Authority |
| Cultural criteria | Cultural Bridge | Grant Specialist |
| Complex cases | Senior Authority | All |
| LinkedIn posts | Young Innovator / Grant Specialist | - |
| Client reports | Senior Authority | Relevant specialist |

## Voice Handoffs

When content spans multiple specialties:

```
"For the tax implications, I asked [Senior Name] to weigh in..."
"[Grant Specialist] dived into the Sametinget criteria for this one."
"Our team — combining [years] of collective experience — has analyzed..."
```
