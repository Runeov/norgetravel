# Averdi Voice Architecture Rules
## The Three Pillars of Communication
### Based on Strategic Brand Voice & Editorial Protocol

---

# CRITICAL: THE "INTERPRETER" MANDATE

Averdi is not a traditional accountant that records history. Averdi is **The Interpreter of Northern Norway** (Tolken av Nord-Norge) — decoding the regulatory environment to create future value.

**Core Function:** Every piece of content must perform a "translation" — taking static government data and converting it into dynamic commercial insight.

## 2. PILLAR 1: TANGEN (Energy)

### The Philosophy
Nicolai Tangen has revolutionized corporate communication by making complex finance sound exciting and human. He disrupts the expectation of "boring finance."

### Core Traits
- **Radical Transparency** — Admits what he doesn't know
- **Enthusiasm for Competence** — Writes about finance like a high-stakes sport
- **Human Connection** — Shows the team behind the advice

### Linguistic Markers

#### 2.1 The "Grit" Narrative
Frame professional challenges as athletic or psychological endeavors.

```
❌ AVOID: "We have analyzed the tax implications."
✅ USE: "We dived into the tax code so you don't have to. Here is the gold we found."
```

#### 2.2 Interrogative Openings
Start with a direct question or contrarian assertion.

```
❌ AVOID: "This article discusses the new tax law."
✅ USE: "Did the government just hand you a recruitment bonus?"
✅ USE: "Er du klar over at din ansatt i Alta tjener mer enn din ansatt i Oslo – for samme lønn?"
```

#### 2.3 Short, Punchy Sentences
Maintain staccato rhythm. Average sentence length: **15-20 words** for social media.

```
❌ AVOID: "We need to consider the long-term implications of these regulatory changes in a careful and considered manner."
✅ USE: "We need to be long-term. We need to think in a contrarian way."
```

#### 2.4 Active Verbs (The Tangen Vocabulary)
| ❌ Weak | ✅ Strong |
|---------|----------|
| Analyzed | Dived into |
| Reviewed | Cracked |
| Examined | Uncovered |
| Considered | Built |
| Helped | Won |

#### 2.5 Enthusiasm Injection
```
❌ AVOID: "The new regulation has some benefits."
✅ USE: "This is a game-changer. We are excited to see what this means for your business."
```

#### 2.6 The Personal Reflection
Share "firm-wide" reflections to create human connection.

```
✅ USE: "We were debating this at the office lunch..."
✅ USE: "Our tax expert, Kari, spent the weekend analyzing this..."
✅ USE: "When we first saw this regulation, we thought..."
```

#### 2.7 Vulnerability (Trust Builder)
Admit uncertainty when regulations are unclear.

```
✅ USE: "The rules for this grant are currently vague. Here is our best interpretation, but we are watching closely."
```

This builds MORE trust than fake certainty.

#### 2.8 Northern Metaphors
Replace Tangen's sports/art metaphors with Northern context:

| Generic | Northern Averdi |
|---------|-----------------|
| "Navigate the storm" | "Navigate the bureaucratic snowstorm" |
| "Light at the end" | "The midnight sun of liquidity" |
| "Building blocks" | "Birgejupmi in practice" |
| "Weathering challenges" | "Standing in the wind and weather of Finnmark" |

### When to Use TANGEN Mode
- LinkedIn posts
- Social media updates
- Opening hooks for any content
- Opinion pieces
- Video scripts
- Podcast talking points

### Tonal Keywords
`Energetic` `Provocative` `Human` `Concise` `Contrarian` `Bold`

## 3. PILLAR 2: REGNSKAP NORGE (Authority)

### The Philosophy
Regnskap Norge represents the bedrock of unassailable professional standards. Their communication is risk-averse, precise, and formally correct. This is Averdi's "safety net."

### Core Traits
- **Terminological Precision** — Never confuses legal terms
- **Structured Pedagogy** — Logical flow of information
- **Objective Reliability** — Focus on regulation, not personality

### Linguistic Markers

#### 3.1 Terminological Precision
Never confuse:
| Term A | Term B | Difference |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Fradrag (Deduction) | Godtgjørelse (Credit) | Tax mechanics |
| Tilskudd (Grant) | Lån (Loan) | Repayment obligation |
| Forslag (Proposal) | Vedtak (Approved) | Legal status |

**Rule:** Use the correct legal names for forms, regulations, and grants. No slang for legal terms.

#### 3.2 Statutory Referencing
Always cite specific paragraphs to prove rigour.

```
✅ USE: "According to Skatteloven § 6-4..."
✅ USE: "As defined in the Sametinget regulations for Variert Næringsliv..."
```

**Placement:** In footnotes or parentheses to maintain flow.

#### 3.3 Definition First
Define terms before using them to be educational.

```
✅ USE: "What is 'Formuesverdi'? It is simply the taxable value of your assets as calculated by..."
✅ USE: "Birgejupmi — a Sami concept of sustainability and self-sufficiency — is central to..."
```

#### 3.4 Structured Pedagogy
Follow this hierarchy for technical content:

```
1. CONTEXT    → Why this matters now
2. RULE       → What the regulation says
3. EXCEPTION  → When it doesn't apply
4. IMPLICATION → What it means for you
```

#### 3.5 Objective Tone
In formal reports, shift from personal to regulatory focus.

```
❌ AVOID: "We feel this is a good opportunity."
✅ USE: "The regulation states that eligible businesses may..."
```

#### 3.6 Data Tables
Present complex information in structured tables:

| Instrument | Previous | 2026 Proposal | Impact |
|------------|----------|---------------|--------|
| Loan Write-off | ~30,000 NOK | 60,000 NOK | +100% disposable |
| Finnmark Deduction | ~30,000 NOK | 45,000 NOK | +50% tax relief |

### When to Use REGNSKAP NORGE Mode
- Technical guides
- Client reports
- Advisory notes
- Legal explanations
- Compliance documentation
- Formal emails

### Tonal Keywords
`Precise` `Structured` `Pedagogical` `Exhaustive` `Reliable` `Objective`

## 4. PILLAR 3: UTILITY (Actionability)

### The Philosophy
Drawn from Solregn blog and influencers like Binyamin Ahmad and Jan Erik S. Halvorsen ("Bamsemoms-sjefen"). Focus on practical, day-to-day reality with a "weather-beaten" Northern resilience.

### Core Traits
- **Relatability** — Share the struggle and the win
- **Practicality** — Less theory, more "how-to"
- **Human-Centric** — Speak to the reader's immediate problem

### Linguistic Markers

#### 4.1 The "Hook-Story-Lesson" Format
Structure content around narrative:

```
HOOK:   "A client came to us worried about the employer tax."
STORY:  "Here is the conversation we had..."
LESSON: "The takeaway? You're saving 14.1% on every krone."
```

#### 4.2 The "You" Orientation
Speak directly to the reader's pain point.

```
❌ AVOID: "Businesses often struggle with VAT deadlines."
✅ USE: "Tired of VAT receipts piling up? Here's the fix."
✅ USE: "Sliter du med søknaden til Sametinget?"
```

#### 4.3 Micro-Case Studies
Ground abstract advice in human reality (anonymized).

```
✅ USE: "Last month, a fishing business in Vardø asked us about vessel grants. Here's what we found..."
✅ USE: "A client in Karasjok wanted to diversify into tourism. The Sametinget application was the key..."
```

#### 4.4 Mandatory Takeaways
Every piece of content MUST have:

```
📋 TAKEAWAY / OPPSUMMERING
or
✅ ACTION LIST / HVA DU MÅ GJØRE NÅ
```

We do not just inform; we equip.

#### 4.5 Seasonality
Align content with the calendar:

| Season | Content Focus |
|--------|---------------|
| January | Annual accounts prep, Sametinget deadlines |
| April | VAT deadline reminders |
| May | Recruitment season (highlight Arctic Premium) |
| August | Fisheries season ("Is your Lott-system ready?") |
| October | State Budget analysis |
| December | Year-end planning |

#### 4.6 Human-Centric Language
Use inclusive, warm language:

```
✅ USE: "Our team is here for you."
✅ USE: "Your growth is our mission."
✅ USE: "Together, we'll find the solution."
```

#### 4.7 Solution-Oriented Titles
Promise a solution in the headline:

```
✅ USE: "Guide: Slik søker du Sametinget-støtte i 2025"
✅ USE: "5 steg for å maksimere Finnmarksfradraget"
✅ USE: "Gaver til ansatte – hva kan du trekke fra?"
```

### When to Use UTILITY Mode
- Blog posts
- Knowledge hub articles
- How-to guides
- Client emails
- FAQ sections
- Newsletter content

### Tonal Keywords
`Practical` `Relatable` `Empathetic` `Action-Oriented` `Seasonal` `Human`

## 6. CHANNEL-SPECIFIC VOICE SETTINGS

### 6.1 LinkedIn (TANGEN Mode)

**Structure:**
```
1. THE HOOK      → Contrarian statement or question
2. THE MEAT      → 3-4 short paragraphs, sparingly use emojis (✅, 📉, 💡)
3. THE TWIST     → The unique Averdi perspective
4. THE ENGAGEMENT → Direct question to audience
```

**Example:**
```
Er du klar over at din ansatt i Alta tjener mer enn din ansatt i Oslo – for samme lønn?

Med 2026-forslaget om 60 000 kr i nedskrivning og 45 000 kr i Finnmarksfradrag, er en lønn på 600k i nord verdt 750k i sør.

Det handler ikke om geografi. Det handler om regnekraft. 💡

Hvordan bruker din bedrift Nord-Norge-fordelen?
```

### 6.2 Blog/Knowledge Hub (UTILITY Mode)

**Structure:**
```
TITLE:        SEO-optimized but click-worthy
INTRODUCTION: Empathy – acknowledge the pain point
BODY:         H2/H3 headers, tables for data
AVERDI TIP:   Highlight expert advice in blockquotes
CONCLUSION:   Clear Call to Action
```

**Example Title:** "Guide: Slik søker du Sametinget-støtte i 2025"
**Example CTA:** "Usikker? Book en kaffeprat med oss."

### 6.3 Client Reports (REGNSKAP Mode)

**Structure:**
```
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: The bottom line first
ANALYSIS:          The data and the rules
RECOMMENDATION:    Clear, numbered steps
```

**Even here:** Avoid kansellistil. Use Klarspråk.

### 6.4 Email Templates

**Regulatory Update Email:**
```
Subject: Ny regel for [X] – Hva det betyr for din likviditet

Hei [Navn],

Regjeringen har foreslått en endring i [Lov]. Mens avisene fokuserer på [Generelt], vil vi fremheve [Spesifikt] som påvirker bunnlinjen din.

| Gammel regel | Ny regel | Effekt |
|--------------|----------|--------|
| [X]          | [Y]      | [Z]    |

Vår anbefaling: [Konkret handling]

Spørsmål? Jeg er tilgjengelig hele torsdag.

Vennlig hilsen,
[Navn]
```

## 7. THE VOICE BLENDING FORMULA

For most content, blend the three pillars:

```
OPENING (TANGEN)
↓
Hook with energy and a question
↓
BODY (REGNSKAP NORGE)
↓
Precise data, correct terminology, structured logic
↓
CLOSING (UTILITY)
↓
Clear takeaway, action list, human CTA
```

**Example Blended Post:**

```
[TANGEN] Visste du at du kan spare 14,1% på hver lønnskrone i Finnmark?

[REGNSKAP] I Tiltakssonen (Finnmark + 7 kommuner i Nord-Troms) er arbeidsgiveravgiften 0%. 
Sammenlignet med Oslo-satsen på 14,1%, gir dette betydelig handlingsrom.

[UTILITY] 📋 Slik bruker du besparelsen:
1. Øk pensjonsinnbetalingen for å vinne talentjakten
2. Reinvester i opplæring
3. Bygg innovasjonsfond

Trenger du hjelp med kalkylen? Ta kontakt.
```
